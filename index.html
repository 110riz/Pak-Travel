Sindh

Explore the cultural hub of Sindh, Karachi, and its iconic landmarks like the Mohenjo-Daro ruins. Indulge in the flavors of Sindhi cuisine while soaking in the beauty of Keenjhar Lake, known as the Jewel of Sindh.

Sindh

Punjab

Discover the historic treasures of Lahore, including the majestic Badshahi Mosque and Lahore Fort. Lose yourself in the vibrant culture of Punjab as you traverse its verdant plains.

Punjab

Balochistan

Embark on an adventure to the remote landscapes of Balochistan, dotted with ancient forts and mystical sites. Witness the surreal beauty of Hingol National Park and experience the nomadic lifestyle of the desert regions.

Balochistan

Khyber Pakhtunkhwa (KPK)

Trek through the rugged terrains of Khyber Pakhtunkhwa and explore the historic Swat Valley. Delve into the rich cultural heritage and savor the flavors of traditional Pashtun cuisine.

Khyber Pakhtunkhwa

Gilgit-Baltistan

Witness the majestic Karakoram Range and explore the ancient Silk Road routes of Gilgit-Baltistan. Admire the turquoise waters of Attabad Lake and immerse yourself in the enchanting beauty of Hunza Valley

Gilgit Baltistan

Kashmir

Embark on a journey to the breathtaking landscapes of Kashmir, where serene lakes and snow-capped peaks await. Experience the tranquility of Shangrila Resort in Skardu or indulge in a shikara ride on Dal Lake in Srinagar.

Azad Jammu Kashmir

Islamabad

Experience the elegance of Pakistan's capital, Islamabad, against the scenic backdrop of the Margalla Hills. Discover architectural marvels like Faisal Mosque and unwind amidst the lush greenery of Shakarparian Hills

Islamabad

Bani Gala

Nestled near Islamabad, Bani Gala, known as Prime Minister Imran Khan's residence, offers serene beauty amidst the Margalla Hills. Explore its lush greenery, pristine lakes, and charming villages—a hidden gem waiting to be discovered.
